Forester

Forester is a idle clicker type of game. The player has to plant and cut down trees to earn wood and money. The player can build houses to house workers, who will then cut down trees for the player.

I made the game along with two other people. I was in charge of the code, and my two other team members did graphics.
We made the game to showcase it at Vectorama, a lan event in Oulu. We had around 5 months to make it, so we had plenty of time to make the game work well and look pretty.

Overall I really enjoyed this project, as I finally had real artists doing graphics, and there were lots of cool I got to make, like the saving and building systems.

Mobile sandbox game

A mobile game was the last school project of the first year. The game is a sandbox/destruction type game, where the player can play around with different blocks and destroy them. I made the game in a group of two, in which I made most of the main mechanics of the game and my friend made all of the graphics, like the textures and UI-elements and also a couple small scripts. For example the grid-system, the block connection script and the mechanics of the weapons were made by me.
